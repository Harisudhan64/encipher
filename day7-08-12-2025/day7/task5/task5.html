Climate Change and Extreme Weather

Climate change continues to cause extreme weather events around the world. Many countries are experiencing heavy rainfall, floods, heatwaves, and rising sea levels. Scientists warn that if carbon emissions are not reduced, these events will become more frequent and dangerous. Governments are focusing on renewable energy and environmental protection to control the impact.

Advances in Artificial Intelligence (AI)

AI technology is growing rapidly and being used in healthcare, education, transportation, and business. New AI tools help doctors detect diseases early, assist students in learning, and improve customer service in companies. While AI provides many benefits, experts also say it must be used safely to avoid job loss and privacy issues.
