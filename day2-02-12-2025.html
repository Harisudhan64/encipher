Air Pollution


Intoduction


Air pollution is the presence of harmful substances in the air. affects humans, animals, plants, and the overall environment. Today, air pollution has become one of the world’s most serious problems.


Major Causes of Air Pollution


There are several major causes of air pollution:
Smoke from vehicles
Emissions from factories
Burning of plastic and waste
Deforestation
Use of chemical sprays


Effects on Human Health

pollution leads to many health issues. People suffer from breathing problems, headaches, coughing, asthma, and even lung diseases. Children and elderly people are the most affected.

Environmental Impact

Air pollution also affects the environment. It damages crops, reduces rainfall, harms animals, and contributes to global warming. Polluted air also makes cities covered with smog, reducing visibility.

Conclusion

pollution is a growing problem, but it can be reduced. Planting trees, using public transport, reducing waste burning, and following clean-energy methods can help. Clean air is essential for a healthy life, and everyone must take responsibility to protect it.
